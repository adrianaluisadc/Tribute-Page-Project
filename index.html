Tribute Page for My Beloved

Ananta Bipal Subedi

The Brave

He had seen the best and the worst, yet he still faces the odd with bravery and courage. I could not ask for more of a man who is not frightened to face the greatest fears in the name of life. The one who isn't afraid to move forward and continue on growing.

The Strong

A thousand storms may try to bring him to shatter, but he remains still and keeps on fighting. Nothing can bring him down because he can always stand up for he is strong and willed to fight.

The Clever

Life may throw him obstacles and rocks on the way, but he knows the other route. His strategies and tactics has made different positive outcomes from what seems to be the impossible. With his mind, no challenges seem to be big to overcome.
