Tribute Page for My Beloved

Ananta Bipal Subedi

The Brave

He had seen the best and the worst, yet he still faces the odd with bravery and courage. I could not ask for more of a man who is not frightened to face the greatest fears in the name of life. The one who isn't afraid to move forward and continue on growing.

The Strong

A thousand storms may try to bring him to shatter, but he remains still and keeps on fighting. Nothing can bring him down because he can always stand up for he is strong and willed to fight.

The Clever

Life may throw him obstacles and rocks on the way, but he knows the other route. His strategies and tactics has made different positive outcomes from what seems to be the impossible. With his mind, no challenges seem to be big to overcome.

Who is Ananta?

A fighter. That's how I would describe My Ananta. People surrounding him doesn't have any idea how many battles he has to face alone and still win. This shows how resilient he is in any circumstances life may play. His heart is pure and soft, but very strong and unbreakable. Personally, I feel safe because of these characteristic he posseseses. I am not afraid to face anything because I have him always on my side.

Moreover, he has a very optimistic spirit no matter the happenings are. He looks at the silver linings. This lifts up the people surrounding him. With the philosophical mindset towards the world, he has an unorthodox ideologies. He appreciates unique people and knows their capabilities and strengths, and help them discover it. People around him are lucky, for they have someone to look up to that would help them get up.

He is a motivated self-taught learner and it makes him grow at his own pace and faster than average people could catch up. I really love the fact how he values learning and knowledge. I could never forget what he once told me. . .that there is no such thing as a person who can't learn because our brain are so much adaptable to learning. Intelligence is not a fixed thing, it can change over time with hard work and motivation to learn.

And last but not the least, his passion towards music touches my heart. Every time Maya gives me serenades, it gives me chills. For he sings from the heart and play different instruments, I could feel the music he is playing. It speaks its own language. There is no such day as no melody, for he makes my life filled with life, music and growth.

To listen to one of his musics, discover his song produced in 2019 entitled "Feri Pani" and know why I admire him as an amazing musician.
